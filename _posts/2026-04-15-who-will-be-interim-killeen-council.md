The Killeen City Council is expected to decide on an interim city manager and is considering Laurie Wilson, Jeff Reynolds, and outgoing City Manager Kent Cagle.

According to the article, the interim date ranges differ by candidate. Wilson and Cagle are listed for limited windows, while Reynolds is also presented as a potential interim option.

The story frames the decision as part of Killeen's city leadership transition and discusses experience and scheduling details included in the city staff report.
