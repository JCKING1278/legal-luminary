KWTX's "Xtra! Xtra! Interviews" segment features a series of discussions highlighting various community events and initiatives in Central Texas. Notable topics include the Waco Civic Theatre's campaign to raise $30,000 for essential sound and lighting upgrades, as well as the opening of the production "Prince of Egypt" on July 17, 2025. 

Other interviews cover a range of community-focused events, such as the Waco Mayor’s Summer Reading Challenge, the Humane Society of Central Texas's Woofstock festival, and the Central Texas Air Fest. The series also includes personal stories from local figures, like Alyssa Riggs and Nate Smith, discussing their beginnings in the community.

These interviews serve to inform residents about local happenings and encourage participation in community events, fostering a sense of connection and engagement among citizens. The ongoing coverage of these initiatives underscores the importance of community involvement and support for local arts and social programs.
