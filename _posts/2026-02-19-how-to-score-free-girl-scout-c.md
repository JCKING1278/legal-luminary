How to score free Girl Scout cookies by donating blood in February
Throughout February, We Are Blood is treating every donor in Central Texas to a free box of cookies while supplies last.
Author: Kate Norum, Jeff Bell, Britny Eubank
Published: 11:38 AM CST February 4, 2026
Updated: 11:38 AM CST February 4, 2026

AUSTIN, Texas — Want to help the community and satisfy your sweet tooth at the same time? Austin nonprofit We Are Blood is offering the perfect trade-off.

If you donate blood at any of We Are Blood's donor centers or mobile drives during February, you can take home a box of Girl Scout cookies while supplies last.

We Are Blood has boxes of Thin Mints, Peanut Butter Sandwiches, Caramel DeLites and Adventurefuls available for donors. The partnership will benefit Girl Scouts of Central Texas troops.

Following the winter storm in January, We Are Blood has been reeling from a critical shortage of donations. The blood bank is always in need of donations and relies heavily on O blood type as it's universal and any patient — regardless of their blood type — can benefit from it. Type O is frequently in short supply and in high demand during shortages.
