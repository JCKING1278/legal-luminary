Throughout February 2026, We Are Blood, a nonprofit organization in Central Texas, is incentivizing blood donations by offering a free box of Girl Scout cookies to each donor, while supplies last. This initiative aims to address a critical shortage of blood donations that has arisen following a significant decline in contributions due to a winter storm in January, which has left the local blood bank in urgent need of support.

Donors can choose from a selection of popular Girl Scout cookie flavors, including Thin Mints, Peanut Butter Sandwiches, Caramel DeLites, and Adventurefuls. This partnership not only seeks to enhance the local blood supply but also supports the Girl Scouts of Central Texas troops, promoting community involvement in both blood donation and scouting activities.

We Are Blood has emphasized the heightened need for O blood type, which is universally compatible and often in high demand. Individuals interested in donating can schedule an appointment or visit one of the donor centers or mobile drives as walk-ins. This campaign presents a timely opportunity for community engagement, allowing residents to contribute to a vital cause while enjoying a cherished treat.
