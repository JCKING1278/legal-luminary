On February 19, 2026, KVUE announced the premiere of a special program titled "Hearts and Heroes: Flooding in the Texas Hill Country," which will air at 6:30 p.m. on KVUE+. This program highlights the devastating floods that struck Central Texas on July 4, 2025, and the community's response to the disaster. The collaboration between KVUE and its sister stations under TEGNA aims to share personal stories of resilience and support among Texans during and after the floods.

The floods had a significant impact on the lives of many residents, leading to lasting changes in the community. The program will showcase how individuals came together to assist one another in the aftermath of the storms, emphasizing themes of solidarity and heroism. Viewers can access the KVUE+ app on various streaming devices, including Roku, Amazon FireTV, and Apple TV, to watch the special and other news coverage.

This initiative not only serves to inform the public about the floods but also aims to foster a sense of community and healing as residents reflect on their experiences and the support they received.
