UPDATE: Bell County Sheriff’s Office says missing teenager found
By Danika Young
Published: Feb. 19, 2026 at 7:07 AM CST|Updated: 12 hours ago

BELL COUNTY, Texas (KWTX) - The Bell County Sheriff’s Office says missing teenager Grace Smith has been found.

No further information was provided.

----- ORIGINAL ARTICLE------------

The Bell County Sheriff’s Office is asking for the public’s assistance to find a missing juvenile, reported missing on late Wednesday, February 18, 2026.

Grace Smith, 13, was last seen wearing a black long-sleeve shirt, red & black plaid pajama bottoms, and brown ‘Ugg’ boots.

Smith is 5′5″ tall and weights about 110 pounds. She has a thin build, brown hair with green tips, and brown eyes.

The girl was last seen in the area of FM 1670, near the neighborhood of Sherwood Shores Seven.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Sheriff’s Office at 254-933-5412 or their local law enforcement agency.
