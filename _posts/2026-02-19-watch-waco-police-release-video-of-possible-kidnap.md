WATCH: Waco police release video of possible kidnapping; suspect sought
Watch: Suspect sought in possible kidnapping and assault investigation in Waco, Texas
By KWTX Staff
Published: Feb. 19, 2026 at 5:44 PM CST|Updated: 2 hours ago

WACO, Texas (KWTX) - The Waco Police Department Major Crimes Unit is attempting to identify the suspect and victim in a possible kidnapping and assault investigation.

Shortly before 6 a.m. on Feb. 17, police officers were called to a business in the 600 Block of Franklin Ave.

The officers arrived and spoke with an employee who said a man entered the store asking them to call the police because someone just “beat him up.”

As the employee was attempting to call, the suspect entered the store, grabbed the victim, and took him back outside while punching him in the head.

Officers continued to canvass the area for individuals matching the descriptions and were unable to locate either party.

Both the victim’s and the suspect’s identities are unknown, police said.
