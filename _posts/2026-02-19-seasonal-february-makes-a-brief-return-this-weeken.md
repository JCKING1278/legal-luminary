Seasonal February makes a brief return this weekend
Patchy frost possible by early next week
By Max Crawford and Sarah Howard
Published: Feb. 19, 2026 at 8:56 AM CST|Updated: 7 hours ago

BRYAN, Texas (KBTX) - The Brazos Valley is about to get a much-needed weather refresh. After stewing in Gulf moisture and dealing with stubborn clouds this week, a strong cold front will sweep through early Saturday morning, bringing cooler, drier air and clearing skies just in time for the weekend.

After a warm and muggy week, this weekend brings a pleasant refresh with two fronts bringing temperatures back to seasonal.(KBTX)
Friday: The Transition Day

Friday will be a transition day as a weak front tries to move into the area. Expect mainly overcast skies and a couple of scattered showers to drift through, but don’t let that alter your end of week plans - any rain that falls will be quite light; barely enough to wet the pavement.
