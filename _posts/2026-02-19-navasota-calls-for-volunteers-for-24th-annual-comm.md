Navasota calls for volunteers for 24th annual community cleanup
(KBTX)
By Tyler Hoskins
Published: Feb. 19, 2026 at 4:23 PM CST|Updated: 3 hours ago

NAVASOTA, Texas (KBTX) - The City of Navasota is asking residents, families, businesses, and community groups to roll up their sleeves for the 24th Annual Trash Off community cleanup on Saturday, April 25, 2026.

The annual event brings volunteers together to remove litter and improve public spaces throughout the city. Participants can volunteer individually or as part of a group.

The city will provide trash bags, latex gloves, and event T-shirts to volunteers. Officials encourage participants to bring friends, family members, co-workers, or civic groups.

Registration required by April 3

Pre-registration is required by Friday, April 3, by 4:00 p.m. to guarantee an event T-shirt. Participants who register after the deadline can still join, but may not receive a shirt.

Those under 18 must be accompanied by a registered adult. The city said that all groups must designate a registered adult as the point of contact. Volunteers must provide their own transportation to cleanup sites.

To register or learn more, residents can call 936-825-6475 or visit www.NavasotaTX.gov/TrashOff.
